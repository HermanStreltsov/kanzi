Kanzi

Firefox extension which goal is to help you improve your vocabulary

Build vocabulary
while surfing the web!

Whenever you encounter a word on the internet that you don't know yet, add it to Kanzi! The next time you see the word, you can hover over it and see its meaning right away!

Add to Firefox

Manage your words in handy dictionary

Kanzi includes a convenient dictionary, in which you can look up for words you've added, edit or delete ones you don't need anymore.
